About Us

Who We Are

VGR Inc. is a leading company specializing in weight lifting and workout plans. Established in 2015, our team of certified fitness professionals has been dedicated to helping people of all ages and fitness levels achieve their health and fitness goals. Our mission is to make a lasting impact on our clients' lives by providing exceptional training, personalized workout plans, and ongoing support.

Our History

VGR Inc. was founded by John Doe, a former professional weightlifter and personal trainer with over 20 years of experience in the industry. John recognized the need for a company that could provide comprehensive and customized weight lifting plans and support for people looking to improve their overall fitness and well-being. Since its inception, VGR Inc. has grown rapidly, expanding its team and services to cater to a diverse clientele.

Our Mission

At VGR Inc., our mission is to empower individuals to take control of their fitness journey by providing expert guidance, tailored workout plans, and ongoing support. We believe that everyone deserves access to quality fitness resources that help them achieve their goals and lead healthier, happier lives. Through our personalized approach, we strive to create a lasting impact on our clients' well-being, helping them become the best version of themselves.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with a VGR Inc. weight lifting plan?

To get started with a weight lifting plan, simply fill out our contact form or give us a call. One of our team members will guide you through the process and set you up with an initial consultation to discuss your goals and preferences.

What kind of support can I expect from VGR Inc.?

At VGR Inc., we pride ourselves on providing exceptional support to our clients. This includes personalized workout plans, one-on-one coaching sessions, nutrition advice, and ongoing communication to help you stay on track and make progress towards your goals.

Can I follow a VGR Inc. weight lifting plan if I'm a beginner?

Absolutely! Our weight lifting plans are designed for people of all fitness levels, including beginners. Our expert trainers will help you learn proper form and technique, and your plan will be tailored to your current fitness level and experience.

Do I need access to a gym to follow a VGR Inc. weight lifting plan?

While having access to a gym can be beneficial, it's not a requirement for following our weight lifting plans. We can create customized workout plans that utilize bodyweight exercises or minimal equipment for those who prefer to work out at home or don't have access to a gym.
